Hospital management system is a software application that is used to manage and streamline the various operations of a hospital or healthcare facility. The system provides a digital platform for managing patient records, appointments, medical histories, billing, inventory management, and other administrative tasks.

The system typically includes modules for different departments, such as the emergency department, pharmacy, laboratory, and radiology, allowing for seamless communication and collaboration between departments. It also enables healthcare providers to access patient data from anywhere, making it easier to provide timely and efficient care.

The hospital management system is designed to improve the overall efficiency of the healthcare facility by automating tasks that were previously done manually, reducing the risk of errors, and enabling faster decision-making. It can also help to reduce costs by optimizing resource utilization and improving the accuracy of billing and invoicing.

Overall, the hospital management system is an essential tool for modern healthcare facilities, helping them to provide high-quality care and improve patient outcomes.
